Welcome to Austin Videos & Games

Austin Video & Games is a family-owned, independent video store with two locations in Austin, Texas. From foreign, cult, and classic films to the latest new releases, Austin Video & Games carries everything from the classic to the latest release. While primarily a rental store, Austin Video & Games has many used and new movies for sale.

We are open 10:30 AM to Midnight Monday through Thursday, 10:30 AM to 2 AM Friday and Saturday, and Noon to Midnight Sunday. Hours may change due to holidays and special occasions. Look for special in-store notices for the very latest on Austin Video & Games.
