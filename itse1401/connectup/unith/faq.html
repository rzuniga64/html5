Frequently Asked Questions about ConnectUp

Services

Q: How is ConnectUp different from other networking sites?

A: Other sites focus on networking with friends OR career networking. At ConnectUp, we realize that your professional and social lives overlap. You can use our services to connect with:

  • friends
  • business associates
  • contacts from college
  • people who live in your neighborhood

Pricing

Q: How much does it cost to join ConnectUp?

A: Nothing! It's free.

Q: How do you make money if you don't charge a fee?

A: When you invite friends and associates to join ConnectUp, they will see a small text ad in the email invitation.

Membership

Q: Can anyone join?

A: Most of our members are under the age of 35, but anyone over the age of 18 can join.

Q: How do I invite people to join?

A: Once you've signed up, you'll see an "Invite" button on every page. Simply click that button and fill out the information.

Privacy

Q: Is my information safe with ConnectUp?

A: Yes! We have a strict privacy policy and never release your information to any other companies.

Q: Who can see my ConnectUp profile?

A: You can choose to make your profile publicly available to anyone or only available to your approved ConnectUp connections.

About the Company

Q: How long has ConnectUp been in business?

A: ConnectUp is a new company but our founders have a combined 25 years of experience working with social networking Web sites.

Q: Anything else I should know about ConnectUp?

A: Yes! We give 5% of our net revenues to the top-rated charities, as identified by Charity Navigator.
