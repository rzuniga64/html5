Welcome!

Welcome to TradeWinds Cafe and Emporium. TradeWinds' guests experience an amazing combination of delicious innovative island cuisine, live music, and fine imported goods in a beautiful, yet casual atmosphere.

We are open for breakfast, lunch, and dinner six days a week. (We take Mondays off to rest and prepare for delivering more great culinary and shopping adventures for you.)

We invite you to browse our menu, look at what's new in our emporium, check out our schedule of live music, and make your reservations online.

Can't wait to see you at TradeWinds! Come for lunch, shop awhile, then join us for dinner and live after-dinner music in our lounge.
